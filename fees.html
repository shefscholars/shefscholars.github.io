Payment Terms

  • Deposit: A non-refundable deposit of $250 is required to secure your spot upon acceptance.
  • Full Payment: The remaining balance must be paid at least 14 days before the camp start date.
  • Early Bird Discount: A 10% discount is available for payments made in full at least 60 days before the camp start date.
  • Payment Methods: We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal.

Refund Policy

  • Full Refund (minus deposit): Available if cancellation is made at least 30 days before the camp start date.
  • 50% Refund (minus deposit): Available if cancellation is made 15-29 days before the camp start date.
  • No Refund: For cancellations made less than 15 days before the camp start date.
  • Medical Emergencies: Refunds may be considered on a case-by-case basis with proper documentation.

Financial Aid

MathElite is committed to making our program accessible to talented students regardless of financial circumstances. Limited need-based scholarships are available.

To apply for financial aid, please indicate your interest during the application process and complete the supplementary financial aid form. All financial aid applications must be submitted at least 60 days before the camp start date.
